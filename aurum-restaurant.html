Our Story

The Beginning

Where Passion
Met Purpose

Aurum was born in 2009 from a single, audacious vision: to create a space where Indian culinary heritage could be expressed with the same reverence, precision, and beauty as the great kitchens of Europe.

Chef Aryan Mehta returned from a decade of training in Paris, Lyon, and Tokyo with a profound appreciation for classical technique and a burning desire to honour the flavours of his homeland through a new, elevated lens.

The name Aurum — Latin for gold — speaks to the pursuit of the highest standard in everything: sourcing, technique, hospitality, and experience.

The Kitchen

Our Cuisine

The Philosophy

Where Heritage
Meets Innovation

Our cuisine is rooted in the extraordinary diversity of India's culinary traditions, reimagined through a contemporary fine dining vocabulary. We do not fuse; we translate — honouring the essence of a dish while elevating its expression to something wholly new.

Each menu evolves with the seasons, driven by what is exceptional at that precise moment. We source saffron from Kashmir, black pepper from Kerala, truffles from Périgord, and seafood from the day's catch at Mumbai's dawn market.

Expect bold, clear flavours; unexpected textures; and a narrative thread that connects every course into a coherent, emotional journey.
